<p>Class 12 Physics, <b>carrying 70 marks in theory in the CBSE board exam is a high scoring subject, but it comes with the maximum number of numerical questions. </b></p>


<ul>
        <li>Most questions in units like <b>Electrostatics, Current Electricity, Optics, and Modern Physics are directly formula-based.</b></li>
        <li><b>Approx 35-35% of the 70 marks theory paper are numericals, which required formula to solve the questions</b></li>
        <li><b>You can get around 15-20 marks of direct formula application based question in Class 12 Physics. </b></li>
</ul>


<p>So to score well, you need to focus more on understanding and remembering the formula as well as the application of the formula in numericals.</p>


<p>As competitive exams like JEE Main and board exams are formula based so, having access to a structured <b>Class 12 Physics Formula sheet </b>is important for quick revision and error-free problem-solving.</p>


<p>This page provides chapter-wise Physics Formula Sheets, covering all important equations, constants, and standard results as per the latest CBSE syllabus.</p>
