<ul>
        <li><b>According to the most recent CBSE curriculum, the NCERT Class 12 Physics curriculum comes in 9 core units with 14 chapters</b>,</li>

</ul>

<h2>NCERT Class 12 Physics FAQs</h2>


<p class="accordio"><b>Ques. What are the most important chapters in Class 12 Physics board?</b></p>


<div class="liv">
<p>Ans. High-weightage chapters are regarded as being optics, Electrostatics, Current Electricity, Magnetism and EMI. Modern Physics Units are also scoring and are relatively easier to revise.</p>
</div>


<p class="accordio"><b>Ques. Which book is best for Class 12 Physics board preparation?</b></p>


<div class="liv">
<p>Ans. NCERT is the most important book which is used in Class 12 Physics board exams. After reading NCERT, exemplar and sample papers should be done.</p>
</div>


<p class="accordio"><b>Ques. Is Class 12 Physics harder than Chemistry or Maths?</b></p>


<div class="liv">
<p>Ans. Class 12 Physics is concept-based and includes many numericals. However, students can manage everything with regular practice and good understanding of formulas and derivations.</p>
</div>


<p class="accordio"><b>Ques. How to improve numerical solving speed in Class 12 Physics?</b></p>


<div class="liv">
<p>Ans. Improving numerical speed requires regular formula revision, solving previous year questions, and practicing mixed-topic sample papers under timed conditions.</p>
</div>
