<h2 id="3">Physics Chapter 1 NCERT: Important Topics of Electric Charges and Fields</h2>


<p>Some of the important topics of Class 12 Physics Chapter 1 are:</p>


<p>An electric charge refers to the property of subatomic particles that leads it to experience a force when they are placed in an electric and magnetic field. Electric Charges are of two types – like charges and unlike charges</p>


<ol>
        <li>Like Charges repel each other.</li>
        <li>Unlike Charges attract each other.</li>
</ol>


<p>An electric charge has 3 fundamental properties: quantization, additive nature, and conservation of electric charge.</p>


<table class="table table-striped style_table">
        <tbody>
                <tr>
                        <th>
                        <ul>
                                <li>Quantization – The total charge of a body denotes the integral multiple of a basic quantum of charge.</li>
                                <li>Additive – This property represents the total charge of a body as an algebraic sum of all the singular charges that act on the system.</li>
                                <li>Conservation – This property expressed that the total charge of a system is not affected with time. Charges can neither be created nor destroyed.</li>
                        </ul>
                        </th>
                </tr>
        </tbody>
</table>

</ul>
